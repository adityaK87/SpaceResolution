FAQ

1 : What things I will get in 3D Design Service?

Answer : You will get 2D drawings with proper Measurements like ‚Äì Floor Plan with Actual and Proposed planning , Furniture Plan, False Ceiling Plan, Electrical Plan & Legend, Wall Elevations, Kitchen Drawing, 3D Images of all rooms including ceilings , complete Estimate & Full Phone Support .

2 : In how many days I will recieve the drawings?

Answer : Within 7-10 working days after the subscription of Design Service.

3 : How many revisions I get in the drawings?

Answer : You will get 2 revisions after the 1st Initial drawing you recieve.

4 : Can I get Design of a single room?

Answer : Yes for single room it is Rs.2,000. (up to 100 sq. ft.)

5: Can I execute my site with these plan & 3D drawings?

Answer: Yes we provide all 2D plans with measurements & 3D images of your site which helps you to execute your site.

6: Do you give phone support while I am executing the site with my contractor ?

Answer: Yes we provide full phone support for free while your work is being executed.

7 : Do you have team of contractors for all type of work?

Answer: Yes we provide all type of contractors for your home. For More details Call +91-9930930964.

8 : Do you design commercial projects also?

Answer: Yes we provide 2D & 3D design for commercial projects like offices, restaurants, shops, clinics, hotels, etc.

9 : I am a contractor can you provide designs for my clients ?

Answer: We can definitely provide you the best designs to win your project. Contact us for more details.

10 : Do you provide 3D design service out of Mumbai?

Answer: We provide 3D design service out of Mumbai also like Pune, Goa, Konkan, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Delhi, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Gujarat and rest of India.

11 : Can you provide 3D design service without visiting my site/home?

Answer: Simply share your house floor plan (layout) with measurements and site photographs, then our designer will call you & discuss about your placements & requirements over the phone & accordingly we will prepare the complete 2D & 3D design, so you can easily execute it at your end with your local contractor.

12 : How much designing fee you charge if my carpet area is below 500 sq.ft?

Answer: If your carpet is below 500 then you have to pay minimum Rs. 6,000 for 3D Design Service.

13 : For how many days I will get this service? 

Answer: You will get 30 days of time period for any kind of revisions, changes & phone support after we send the 1st initial drawings. After 30 days we won't provide you any type of service or support for the drawings. (No refund)
